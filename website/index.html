Intro

Are you interested in wine but never had the motivation to learn about it because it is too complicated?

Are you willing to learn wine in a fun and simple way?

Then grab a glass, a bottle of wine and let's dive into wine tasting together!

BASICS 01

Lets get started ! First pour yourself some red wine. When filling it up, try to aim for the widest part of your glass. This will maximize the surface in contact with the air, and will help to bring out the perfume and aromas.

When you're satisfied with the quantity, you may scroll down to the next step.
